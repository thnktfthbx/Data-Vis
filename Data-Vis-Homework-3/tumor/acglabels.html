Alphabetic List of Variables and Attributes
Variable Definition
ACG Score Member's locally-weighted ACG risk score (1.0 is local mean).
Active Ingredients The number of medications used by the patient based on ACG data.
Chronic Conditions The number of chronic conditions the patient has based on ACG data.
Coordination Flag A marker to stratify patients based upon the ACG likelihood of coordination issues. 1: LCI = Likely. 0: UCI = “Unlikely, PCI = Possibly.
DxRx Predictive Model ACG predictive model's probability score for high total cost: the probability that this patient will have high total costs (including pharmacy costs) in the year following the observation period.
Group "Line of Business": Commercial, Medicare, or Medicaid. Does not appear on parallel coordinates plot.
Hospital Primary hospital for that patient. Does not appear on parallel coordinates plot.
id Patient's identification number. Does not appear on parallel coordinates plot.
IP Hospital Count The number of inpatient admits the patient has had in the past 12 months.
IP Probability ACG probability score for inpatient admit: the probability that the member will have an inpatient admit in the year following the observation period.
RUB Aggregations of ACGs based upon estimates of concurrent resource that is used to provide a way of separating the population into broad co-morbidity groupings. This measure is useful for resource utilization planning but is not useful for medical assessment purposes.
Untrt Diabetes Flag Flag for whether the member is potentially untreated for a diabetic condition based on ACG data. 1: member has at least one flag for Rx treatment gaps for diabetes. This could be a flag of 'Y', 'D', or 'P', or a CSA or MSR value of <= 0.70. (See the ACG Technical Manual for more details.) 0: member does not appear to be untreated for diabetes.

Reminder: this is based on FAKE data created using random numbers.
